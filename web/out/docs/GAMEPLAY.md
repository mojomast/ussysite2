# Gameplay

## Jump Gates And Hyperspace

Jump gates are physical in-world transit rings. Fly within `12u` of a gate and press `J` to activate its first linked outbound gate. Autopilot can route non-hyperspace pilots through the low-cost gate network.

`HYPERSPACE DRIVE` is a high-tier skill unlock requiring `weap_3` and costing `5` skill points. With a nav target set, press `H` to charge, jump directly to the target, and start a 60 second cooldown. Pilots without hyperspace use gate-network routing for distant travel.
